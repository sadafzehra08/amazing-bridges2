Golden gate bridge

"An iconic symbol of San Francisco, the Golden Gate Bridge is a marvel of engineering that spans 1.7 miles across the Golden Gate Strait. Completed in 1937, this suspension bridge is a testament to human ingenuity and a must-visit attraction for anyone traveling to the City by the Bay."

Brooklyn Bridge

"Spanning the East River, the Brooklyn Bridge is a historic icon that connects Brooklyn and Manhattan. Completed in 1883, this majestic bridge is a masterpiece of 19th-century engineering, offering stunning views of the NYC skyline and a glimpse into the city's rich history."

Tower bridge

"London's iconic Tower Bridge is a majestic fusion of Victorian engineering and Gothic architecture. Spanning the River Thames, this 1886-built bridge is a symbol of the city's rich history and a testament to British ingenuity, offering breathtaking views of the London skyline."
